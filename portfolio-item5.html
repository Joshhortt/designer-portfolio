The story behind this project

Make-UP

Laura is a former dancer, so not only is she absolutely gorgeous, she's extremely stylish too! Everything about her and Thibault's day was beautiful, from Laura's dress to Thibault's suit to the florals.

Juliana set Laura's hair in hot rollers to achieve a silky smooth style with body & curls. Once the hair was set, Laura went to work with my friend Katie Nova (who glams the Ladies on MTV).

After make up Juliana took her to set down and added a full set of blond extensions that were pre prepped for long lasting wear. This style was glamourous and lasted all night on Laura.
